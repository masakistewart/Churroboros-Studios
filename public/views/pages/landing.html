A VR INDIE STUDIO DEDICATED TO CREATING SPICY ORIGINAL CONTENT

Founded by a diverse group of gamers in the San Francisco Bay Area, Churroboros is built upon the common belief that virtual reality content can be far more immersive and competitive than what current platforms support. Recognizing the VR content gap, the developers at Churroboros produced a game that allows players to travel through mind bending scenerios and compete against one another in a highly competitive online multiplayer experience.
